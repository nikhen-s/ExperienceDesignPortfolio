We utilized the Generative Matrix to generate several ideas on products that could support the process of upcycling. In the end, we chose idea B: Reverse Vending Machine for Unused Clothes.


Solution

Reverse Vending Machine for Unused Clothes

For most users, recycling is an extra step which they may find inconvenient. When it comes to recycling clothing, it is quite challenging as skills are required to sow, stitch and repurpose it. This is where our solution comes into place - a reverse vending machine that transforms unused clothing into functional items made of fabric with enough material. This makes the recycling experience convenient - all that a user needs to do is bring their clothing and put it into this machine, no sowing skills are needed. The machine allows the user to choose a fabric item they want to upcycle their clothing into and outputs said fabric item. This discourages them from purchasing a new version that item they need, making it sustainable.
