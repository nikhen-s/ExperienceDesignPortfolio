Week 1, Mobility.

Problem Statement

An elderly woman faces discomfort and potential hazards while waiting for a delayed bus in harsh winter conditions due to the lack of adequate shelter at the bus stop.

Persona

//71 year old female waiting at a bus stop, it is winter around her, she is wearing a yellow puffer jacket at night


Solution

Heated Bus Stop

The proposed solution features an indoor bus stop designed to shield occupants from winter's cold winds, incorporating glass walls, a heater, heated seats, and optimal lighting, all powered by an environmentally friendly solar panel on the rooftop. This solution provides an efficient and sustainable way to enhance the user experience of waiting for a bus, which would incentivize people to use public transportation despite the cold weather.
