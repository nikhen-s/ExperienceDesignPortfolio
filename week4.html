Week 4, Style.

Problem Statement

Source: Patent No: US3423832A

Orthodontic headgear is essential for correcting dental issues in children. However, children often resist wearing headgear due to its unappealing appearance. To address this issue, we are exploring ways to make headgear more stylish, incorporating fun colors, customizable designs, and comfortable materials.

Exploration of Style.


Solution

Well Fashioned Orthodontic Helmet

The primary objective of the well-fashioned orthodontic helmet with a bracelet is to provide effective orthodontic treatment for individuals in need of braces while minimizing the visibility and invasiveness of traditional orthodontic appliances. It aims to enhance the aesthetics of orthodontic treatment by integrating seamlessly with the user's style, including their clothing choices. The design also focuses on improving user comfort and experience during orthodontic treatment. Overall, the conscious choice of style and expression in the design is driven by a desire to make orthodontic treatment more appealing, less invasive, and in line with the preferences and sensibilities of the target users, ultimately improving their overall orthodontic experience.
